# Karis Furniture: Redefining Digital Interior Retail

A sophisticated furniture showcase and e-commerce platform built for the modern shopper. Karis Furniture combines a minimalist, high-end design with a powerful full-stack backend to provide an immersive shopping experience.

## The Vision

The goal was to create a digital showroom that feels as premium as the furniture it displays. By leveraging Next.js and TypeScript, I ensured a blazingly fast, type-safe environment that handles complex product data with ease.
